================================================================================

Reset: rst_n active-low (when present).

5. FUNCTIONAL BEHAVIOR
================================================================================
Increment frame_cnt on rx_frame or tx_frame pulse (+64 bytes each). Increment err_cnt on rx_err or tx_err. clear resets all counters.
