Verification status: PASS

2. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================
FR-001: Module shall implement 32-bit barrel shifter: SLL (op=00), SRL (op=01), SRA (op=10). Default op=11 passes a through..
FR-002: Outputs shall be purely combinational with no clock or reset dependency.
FR-003: All port widths shall match the locked specification exactly.
FR-004: Functional relation: Combinational. op=2b00 SLL, op=2b01 SRL, op=2b10 SRA (arithmetic), op=2b11 pass-through. All outputs purely combinationa.
FR-005: Combinational. op=2b00 SLL, op=2b01 SRL, op=2b10 SRA (arithmetic), op=2b11 pass-through. All outputs purely combinational.
