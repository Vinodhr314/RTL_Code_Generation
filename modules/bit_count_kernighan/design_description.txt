5. FUNCTIONAL BEHAVIOR
================================================================================
On start: FSM runs bit_count_kernighan on hardcoded test data. After computation, asserts done=1, irq=1, result=32'd16. Busy held high during computation.

RTL header notes:
- bit_count_kernighan — algorithm accelerator stub (Verilog-2005)
- Popcount of 0x55AA55AA = 16
- result = 32'd16
- FSM: IDLE → BUSY (10 cycles) → DONE; irq pulses one cycle.

FSM states (from RTL localparams):
- IDLE → BUSY_ST → DONE_ST
