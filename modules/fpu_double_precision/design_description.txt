================================================================================
IEEE-754 double-precision FPU stub (add/sub/mul/div)..

Integration context:
- Part of the RV32 platform address map and interrupt fabric
- Follows SoC coding_style.md (Verilog-2005, rst_n active-low where clocked)

Verification status: PASS; Float64 FADD stub: 1.0+2.0=3.0 after 8 cycles

================================================================================

FR-003: Interface signaling shall match tb/<name>_tb.v drive/monitor assumptions.
FR-004: Multi-cycle stub: FADD returns 1.0+2.0=3.0 (64'h4008000000000000) after 8 cycles.
