4. TIMING, CLOCKING & RESET
================================================================================
Clock/reset: refer to port list and RTL always blocks.

================================================================================
5. FUNCTIONAL BEHAVIOR
================================================================================
Generates gated/divided clocks for core (div1), bus (div2), and peripherals (div4) from clk_ref. pll_lock asserts after cfg_wr latches cfg. cfg[3:0]=cpu div, cfg[7:4]=bus div, cfg[11:8]=periph div (0=pass-through).

RTL header notes:
- cmu — Clock management unit (Verilog-2005)
- Divides clk_ref for cpu (div1), bus (div2), periph (div4). pll_lock on cfg_wr.
