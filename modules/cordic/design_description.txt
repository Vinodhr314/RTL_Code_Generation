================================================================================
FR-001: Module shall implement CORDIC rotator stub: 16-iteration rotation producing hardcoded cos(45deg) output..
FR-002: Assert busy after start; deassert when done.
FR-003: Present final result on result port when done=1.
FR-004: Pulse irq for one cycle coincident with completion (unless RTL notes otherwise).
FR-005: base_addr and length may be ignored in stub model — document actual RTL behavior.

4. TIMING, CLOCKING & RESET
================================================================================
Clock domain: single clk.
Reset: rst_n active-low (when present).

================================================================================
5. FUNCTIONAL BEHAVIOR
================================================================================
On start: runs 16 CORDIC iterations then asserts done. x_out=32'h5A827999, y_out=32'h5A827999 (Q1.30 cos/sin 45deg approx).

RTL header notes:
- cordic — 16-iteration CORDIC rotator stub (Verilog-2005)
- Produces hardcoded cos/sin 45deg outputs after 16 cycles.

FSM states (from RTL localparams):
- IDLE → BUSY → DONE → X_RESULT → Y_RESULT
